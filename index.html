StudyFlow helps students manage courses, deadlines, notes, study sessions, GPA goals, and academic priorities in one organized place.

Built for real student problems

Students often lose track of assignments, exams, notes, and study plans because academic work is spread across different apps, notebooks, and reminders.

  • Deadlines are easy to miss when courses become busy.
  • Notes can become hard to find later in the semester.
  • Students may not know which course needs the most attention.
  • Study plans often fail when they are not connected to actual tasks.

What StudyFlow will help you manage

Semesters

Organize your academic year by creating semesters and tracking which one is currently active.

Courses

Keep course details, instructors, schedules, credit hours, grades, and related tasks in one place.

Tasks and deadlines

Track assignments, quizzes, exams, projects, readings, labs, and custom academic tasks.

Notes

Create course-specific notes and mark important information for quick review later.

Study planner

Plan study sessions and connect them to courses or upcoming deadlines.

Dashboard

View today’s tasks, upcoming deadlines, overdue work, high-priority tasks, and courses needing attention.

How StudyFlow will work

  1. Create your account.
  2. Set up your current semester.
  3. Add your courses.
  4. Add your deadlines, notes, and study sessions.
  5. Use your dashboard to decide what needs attention first.

Your future academic command center

The StudyFlow dashboard will show a clear overview of your academic workload so you can focus on the right task at the right time.

  • Today’s tasks
  • Upcoming deadlines
  • Overdue tasks
  • High-priority work
  • Courses needing attention
  • Recently updated notes
  • Study sessions for today
  • GPA goal progress
