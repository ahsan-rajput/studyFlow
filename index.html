Why StudyFlow exists

Built for real student problems

Academic work becomes difficult to manage when assignments, notes, deadlines, and study plans are spread across too many places.

Missed deadlines

Important assignments and exams are easy to miss when multiple courses become busy at the same time.

Scattered notes

Course notes can become hard to find later in the semester when they are not connected to the right class.

Unclear priorities

Students need a simple way to understand which course, task, or deadline deserves attention first.
